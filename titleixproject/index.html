What is Title IX

Title IX is an education amendment which prohibits discrimination based on sex assigned at birth, gender identity, sexuality, race/ethnicity, immigration status, and disabilities at federally funded education institutions. Under Title IX, acts of sexual assault, violence and harrassment are prohibited
